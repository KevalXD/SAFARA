About SAFARA

Safety-Aware Navigation
for Everyone.

SAFARA combines community incident reports, environmental signals, and intelligent routing to guide users through the safest paths — not just the fastest.

🛡
Safety First

Unlike standard navigation, SAFARA prioritizes your safety over speed. Dangerous zones are processed internally — you only see calm, reassuring route guidance.

🤖
AI-Powered Routing

NLP models classify incident reports by severity. The routing engine factors real-time safety scores to recommend the best path for every user.

👥
Community Driven

Every report from every user improves safety for everyone. Verified incidents silently update routes — protecting the next commuter automatically.

🔒
Privacy by Design

All reports are anonymous. No personal data stored. SAFARA only uses aggregated safety signals — never individual user data.

How SAFARA Works
1
User selects a route

Start and destination are submitted to the SAFARA routing engine.

2
Safety data analyzed

Community reports are processed internally through our multi-level verification system. Users never see danger zones.

3
Safest route recommended

Only the safest available route is shown with a calm safety score — no alarming warnings shown to users.

4
NGO verification layer

Reports are verified by trusted NGO partners before affecting routing. Reputation system prevents abuse.

5
Routes update silently

When incidents are confirmed, routes recalculate in the background. Users receive a calm reassuring update.
